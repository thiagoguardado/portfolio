Rubber Monsters: Bath Time

Game JamArcadePhysics

For Global Game Jam 2020, I joined some friends to make a fast and casual joyful arcade game, called "Rubber Monsters: Bath Time".

We built the game from scratch in less than 48 hours. We were thrilled when Rubber Monsters was selected as one of the local site highlights that year.

In this game, you can click and hold to create a vortex that pulls together toy parts inside a bath tub. Your goal is to fulfill a sequence of missions, consisting in pairing toys together.

Play it online or download it on the game's itch.io.page!
