Wizavior (alpha version)

ArcadeFlying GoatsPCUnity

Wizavior (alpha version) is an arcade-style PC-game concept inspired by Atari’s Frostbite.

This project started as an academic project but quickly became something bigger.

Wizavior (alpha version) tells the story of a wizard, capable of conjuring portals using the power of magic crystals, who tries to save aliens from the imminent destruction of their home planet.

The player controls the wizard, jumping from platform to platform and returning to the central platform to energize the portal with collected crystals. Each level lasts 45 seconds, and during this time the player must repeat that loop to keep the portal open and allow the aliens to escape to the wizard’s planet.

The more energy unloaded in portal, more points the player gets. Also, the larger the portal is at the end of each level, bigger is the additional points given.

The difficulty raises up as velocity increases and obstacles emerge!

Controls: Keyboard/Controller
