Mesmerized

Can you heal them all?

Mesmerized is a multiplatform puzzle-platform game, best played on tablets.

You play as Alex, a mind doctor in a steampunk universe. Enter the subconscious of her patients to explore unique dioramas as you solve challenging puzzles. Collect especial items and find a way to heal them all.

Mesmerized is a final project for Development of Digital Games specialization course at PUCRS. It is a collaborative piece of work, made by the incredible team below in 3 months.

Vivianne Predebon - 2D Artist
Jessica Azambuja - 3D Artist
Natalia Machado - Programmer
