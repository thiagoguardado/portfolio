Chiiip

BrowserGame JamLinuxmacOSUnityWindows

Sacrifices must be made

Ludum Dare 43

Chiiip is a browser/standalone game made in a weekend as part of Ludum Dare 43.

Its a fast-paced brawl arena, where you, controlling the sheep Chiip, fight for your life against starving wolves.

Just like in every average modern family, real-time discussions happen in the family messenger group ("Baaaaassenger").

It's a collaborative piece of work, made by:
