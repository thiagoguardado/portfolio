PewPew Pillow

Game JamUnityWindows

PewPew Pillow is a game made for 2019 Global Game Jam. The game was developed in Unity and I worked as programmer and 2D animator.

The game is a journey through a kid's imagination. Don't let the fantasy fade into the real world. Keep the adventure going by defeating the dangerous enemies inside the house. Beat magic creatures to collect materials to build up your pillow fort, until it is strong enough…
