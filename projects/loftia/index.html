Loftia

Unreal EngineMMOGameplay SystemsPC

Loftia is an in-development cozy, solarpunk MMO by Qloud Games. I joined the team in May 2024 as an engineer, working mainly on gameplay and systems implementation in Unreal Engine.

The game centers on social multiplayer play across floating islands, with cooperative activities like exploration, crafting, housing, farming, and community projects.

My Role

  • Implemented gameplay features and core systems using Unreal Engine.
  • Contributed to multiplayer-facing systems supporting cooperative and social play.
  • Worked with design to translate feature goals into production-ready game behavior.

Project Context

Loftia is planned for release on Steam in 2026, and development updates are shared regularly through official channels.
