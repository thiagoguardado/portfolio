Wizavior (under development)

UnityPCAction Platformer

Wizavior is an action platformer 3d game, where you help the wizard Zavior on his quest to save planets and its inhabitants from imminent destruction.

On his quest, Zavior will face a variety of threats and battle the evil force seeking destruction.

[caption id=“attachment_221” align=“aligncenter” width=“480”] Different planets stand on Zavior’s quest![/caption]

A wizard, a savior... Wizavior!
Wizavior is now under devolpment for PC, using Unity game engine and is set to be released on late 2017.

In this project, I act as lead programmer and game designer and I have been involved in all stages of development, from conception, game design document elaboration, production and testing.

You can find more information on the game’s page on Facebook!

The project is a collaboration between:

  • Thiago Guardado Beltran - - lead programmer
  • Henrique Andrade - lead modeler / vfx designer
  • Douglas Nepumuceno Dias - lead 2D artist
  • Douglas Polo - lead animator
  • Rodrigo Rebelatto - lead audio designer
  • Split Studio - narrative, character design, 2d animations, support
