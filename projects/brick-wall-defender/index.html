Brick Wall Defender

BrowserArcadeLocal Multiplayer

Brick Wall Defender is a simple browser prototype that you can play alone or with a friend locally.

This prototype is mostly inspired by “Oink!”, the classic game from Activision, which debuted on Atari on 1983.

In “Oink!”, a wolf destroys the pig’s house floor brick by brick. Your objective is to rebuild the bricks so the wolf cannot reach the pigs.

In Brick Wall Defender, you play a similar role, except the walls are rotating around you, as well as the enemy. To rebuild a brick, you simply point towards it and press the action button, as long as you have ammo, that constantly spawns around you.

As you progress, the enemy gets faster and action intensifies.

The goal is to reach the highest level possible.

You can play it right now on itch.io!
