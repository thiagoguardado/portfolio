Ghosts 'n Goblins - Remake

UnityWindows

This project is an attempt to recreate Ghosts ‘n Goblins original arcade game, launched at 1985, as close to original as possible.

The original game has been ported to 17 different platforms and has had a bunch of remakes through the last years.

My goal was to recreate the game trying to capture all original visuals and feel, using its original art and music.

I implemented the first level, including animations, music, sound effects, enemies, weapons, itens, score and life system. Also, i made it multiplayer, up to 2 players, just like the original.

The most difficult job was to recreate enemiews behaviour. I spent hours analysing the original game to make enemies that act and feel like the originals.

The project is hosted at itch.io and you can download using the link below.
